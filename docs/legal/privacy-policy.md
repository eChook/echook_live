# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **eChook** ("**we**", "**us**") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the eChook Live telemetry dashboard at [live.echook.uk](https://live.echook.uk) and related services at [data.echook.uk](https://data.echook.uk).

## 1. Who we are

**Data controller:** eChook  
**Contact:** [info@echook.uk](mailto:info@echook.uk)  
**Location:** United Kingdom

For how **telemetry data** is owned, stored, and controlled, see our [Data Management Policy](/#/data-management).

## 2. What personal data we collect

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Email address** | Account identification and verification codes for profile changes |
| **Car name** | Login identifier and display on dashboards and spectator views |
| **Team name** | Display and association with your car |
| **Car number** | Display and race identification |
| **Password** | Stored only as a secure hash; never in plain text |
| **Account metadata** | Account creation date, last login, spectator opt-out preference |
| **Session cookie** | Keeps you logged in (essential for the service) |

We do **not** use advertising cookies or third-party tracking analytics.

**Browser storage:** Unit preferences, graph settings, and local analytics are stored in your browser's local storage on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

**Telemetry data** (voltage, current, GPS, speed, lap data, etc.) is described in our [Data Management Policy](/#/data-management). Where telemetry can be linked to an identifiable team member, it may constitute personal data under applicable law.

## 3. How we use your data

We use account data to:

- Provide and maintain your account and login session
- Send one-time verification codes when you change sensitive account details
- Operate spectator features according to your opt-out preference
- Maintain service security and prevent abuse

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 4. Legal bases (UK GDPR / EU GDPR)

| Processing | Legal basis |
|------------|-------------|
| Creating and running your account | **Contract** — necessary to provide the service you requested |
| Session cookies | **Legitimate interests** — essential for authentication |
| Email verification codes | **Contract** — necessary to verify account changes |
| Security and abuse prevention | **Legitimate interests** — protecting the platform and users |

## 5. Cookies

eChook Live uses **essential cookies only** — a session cookie required for login and authenticated API access. We do not use advertising or analytics tracking cookies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling session cookies will prevent you from staying logged in. Browser local storage is used for preferences (see Section 2) and is not governed by cookie consent rules as it is not used for tracking.

## 6. Who we share data with

We do not sell or rent personal data. We use the following **sub-processors** to operate the service:

| Sub-processor | Role |
|---------------|------|
| eChook Server hosting | Account storage, telemetry storage, API and WebSocket services |
| Cloudflare | Encrypted backups of server data |
| GitHub Pages | Hosting of the eChook Live frontend |
| OpenStreetMap / tile providers | Map display (your browser requests tiles directly; no personal data is sent by us) |

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of eChook, users, or others.

**Telemetry visibility:** Telemetry associated with your car ID can be retrieved via public API endpoints without login. Anyone who knows your car ID can access your stored telemetry and location data. See the [Data Management Policy](/#/data-management) for full details and how to opt out of spectator mode.

## 7. Data retention

| Data type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Account data** | Retained while your account is active; deleted when you delete your account (see Section 9) |
| **Telemetry** | Stored at full resolution while on our servers. **We make no guarantee how long telemetry will remain available** and may delete it at any time. Export your data regularly if you need a permanent record. |
| **Verification codes** | Short-lived; discarded after use or expiry |
| **Browser local storage** | Controlled by you; cleared when you clear site data |

## 8. International transfers

Our infrastructure may process data in the United Kingdom and through sub-processors that operate globally (e.g. Cloudflare). The UK has received an EU adequacy decision, meaning data flows between the UK and EEA do not require additional safeguards. For other international transfers we rely on appropriate safeguards as required.

## 9. Your rights

If UK GDPR or EU GDPR applies to you, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of personal data we hold
- **Rectification** — correct inaccurate data (via Settings or by contacting us)
- **Erasure** — request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Restriction** — ask us to limit processing in certain circumstances
- **Portability** — receive account data in a structured, commonly used format
- **Object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent (note: core account processing is contract-based, not consent-based)

**How to exercise your rights:** Email [info@echook.uk](mailto:info@echook.uk). We will respond within one month, or let you know if we need more time.

You may also lodge a complaint with the **UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)** at [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk). EU residents may additionally contact their local supervisory authority.

**In-app controls:**

- Update profile details in **Settings**
- Export telemetry as CSV from the dashboard
- Delete server-stored telemetry via **Settings → Delete Server Telemetry**
- Delete your account via **Settings → Delete Account**

## 10. Young people and children

Greenpower racing teams frequently include young people and school groups. eChook Live accounts are **per-car** and should be registered and managed by an **adult team supervisor or responsible person** (aged 18 or over). If you are under 18, you should use this service only under the supervision of an adult within your school's or club's existing data protection arrangements.

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 13 without parental or guardian consent. If you believe a child's personal data has been submitted without appropriate oversight, please contact [info@echook.uk](mailto:info@echook.uk) and we will address it promptly.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. For **material changes** that affect how we use your personal data, we will take additional steps to bring the update to your attention before it takes effect. Continued use of the service after non-material updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
