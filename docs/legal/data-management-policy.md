# Data Management Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

This Data Management Policy explains how **telemetry and race data** uploaded to eChook is stored, accessed, and used. It applies to all teams using eChook telemetry boards and the eChook Live dashboard.

For account-related personal data (email, login, cookies), see our [Privacy Policy](/#/privacy).

## 1. Overview

When your car uploads telemetry to eChook Server, that data is stored on the eChook platform. This policy sets out:

- What licence you grant eChook over your data
- What control your team retains
- How eChook may use the data
- How data is stored, shared, and retained

## 2. Data ownership and licence

Your team **retains ownership** of the telemetry data generated by your car and hardware.

By uploading telemetry to eChook, you grant eChook a **worldwide, royalty-free licence** to store, process, and use that data for the purposes described in Section 4. This licence continues only while your data remains on our servers, and ends when you delete your data or account (except for residual backup copies — see Section 8).

## 3. Your team's operational control

Your team maintains practical control over your car's telemetry at all times:

| Right | How to exercise it |
|-------|-------------------|
| **View** | Log in to eChook Live to see live and historical data |
| **Download** | Export telemetry as CSV from the dashboard (lap data, history, analytics events) |
| **Delete** | Remove all server-stored telemetry via **Settings → Delete Server Telemetry** |

Deleting telemetry is **permanent and irreversible**. Your account remains active unless you separately delete it.

Contact [info@echook.uk](mailto:info@echook.uk) if you need assistance exercising these rights.

## 4. How eChook may use your data

The licence granted in Section 2 allows eChook to use uploaded data for:

- **Operating the service** — storing, displaying, and transmitting telemetry to your dashboard
- **Development and debugging** — improving eChook hardware, firmware, server software, and the Live dashboard
- **Aggregate analysis** — publishing statistical summaries and anonymised or aggregated insights derived from telemetry across many cars and teams

**eChook will never share, publish, or disclose data that identifies an individual team, car, or race session without that team's prior explicit permission.**

Aggregate analysis means combined statistics where individual teams cannot be identified — for example, fleet-wide average efficiency trends. It does not include publishing your raw telemetry, lap times attributed to your team, or identifiable race traces without your consent.

## 5. Public access and spectator mode

> **Important:** Telemetry data, including GPS location, can be accessed by anyone who knows your car ID without logging in. Read this section carefully.

### Public API (unauthenticated)

Telemetry for your car can be retrieved **without any login** via:

- Latest data: `/api/get/{carId}`
- Historical data: `/api/history/{carId}`

Your car ID is shown in **Settings**. Treat it as a capability credential — anyone with the ID can access your stored telemetry, including GPS location history, via these endpoints. If you want to prevent public access to your historical data, you should delete your server telemetry (see Section 3).

### Spectator mode

When three or more cars on the same known track have **not** opted out, a subset of live data (car name, team, speed, and live location) may appear on the public spectator view at live.echook.uk.

**Opt out** at any time via **Settings → Spectator View Opt-Out**. Note that opting out of spectator mode does not restrict access via the public API to someone who already knows your car ID.

## 6. Data we collect

Telemetry uploaded from your eChook board may include:

- **Electrical:** voltage, current, amp-hours, power, split-battery voltages
- **Mechanical:** speed, RPM, throttle, gear, brake
- **Environmental:** temperature sensors
- **Location:** GPS latitude and longitude
- **Race context:** track name, lap number, lap summary statistics
- **Timestamps** for each sample

Derived metrics calculated by the dashboard (e.g. power, energy used) are computed from the above.

## 7. Retention

Telemetry is stored at the **full resolution received** from your car while it remains on our servers.

**eChook makes no guarantee how long telemetry will be kept.** Data may be deleted at any time without notice, for example due to storage limits, maintenance, or operational needs.

Teams who need a permanent record should **export their data regularly** using the CSV export features in the dashboard.

Account deletion (see Privacy Policy) removes your account and all associated telemetry.

## 8. Backups

eChook may maintain server backups (including optional Cloudflare R2 backups operated by administrators). Deleted telemetry will be removed from active storage immediately; residual copies in backups may persist until those backups rotate or are overwritten.
